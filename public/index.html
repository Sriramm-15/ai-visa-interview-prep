📘 Tips for a Successful Visa Interview After a Refusal Attempt

  • ✅ Review your previous refusal reason and be ready to explain what has changed
  • ✅ Ensure your updated DS-160 confirmation page is accurate and error-free
  • ✅ Have your new I-20 and SEVIS Fee Receipt (if applicable)
  • ✅ Show stronger or clearer financial documentation to address previous concerns
  • ✅ Practice confident and composed answers—learn from past mistakes
  • ✅ Dress formally, arrive early, and maintain positive body language
  • ✅ Be fully prepared to explain your course, university choice, and funding clearly
  • ✅ Keep your answers honest, consistent, and to the point
